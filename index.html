Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Digital Phenotyping Mental-Health Monitoring Application ("Application", "we", "our", or "us") collects, processes, stores, protects, and shares personal data.

The Application is designed for academic research and clinical analysis by authorized healthcare professionals and researchers to identify:

  • Mental health risk indicators
  • Digital behavioral risks
  • Substance-use behavioral indicators
  • Mobility-behavior correlations

We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection and health-data regulations including GDPR (EU), HIPAA (US), CCPA (California), and Qatar PDPPL.

By using this Application, you acknowledge and consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Categories of Data We Collect

2.1 Behavioral Telemetry Data

  • Screen on/off timestamps
  • Device unlock frequency
  • Session duration
  • Application usage statistics (categories and screen time only)
  • Frequency and duration of digital activity

We do NOT collect:

  • Message content
  • Emails
  • Photos or videos
  • Audio recordings
  • Contact lists
  • Keystroke content

2.2 Location Data (GPS)

  • Approximate GPS coordinates
  • Location timestamps
  • Aggregated mobility movement patterns

Location data is used strictly for behavioral-health analysis and is not sold or used for advertising. GPS access may operate in foreground and background only with explicit consent.

2.3 Motion & Sensor Data

  • Accelerometer data
  • Gyroscope data
  • Pedometer (step count)
  • Physical activity patterns

2.4 Notification Metadata

  • Notification timestamps
  • Interaction events (opened/dismissed)
  • Notification category
  • Notification app name

We do NOT collect notification content.

2.5 Device & System Information

  • Battery level
  • Charging status
  • Device name
  • Technical device information required for system functionality

2.6 Wearable & Health Data (If Connected)

  • Heart rate
  • Oxygen saturation
  • Sleep patterns
  • Blood pressure
  • Respiratory rate
  • Steps
  • Weight

3. Purpose of Data Processing

  • Clinical Analysis: Behavioral analysis by authorized healthcare professionals.
  • Academic Research: Anonymized and aggregated research studies.
  • Automated Risk Detection: Machine learning analysis for professional review.
  • Statistical Insights: Generation of anonymized research reports.
Your data is never used for advertising, marketing, commercial resale, or commercial profiling.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Processing is conducted in compliance with GDPR Articles 6, 7, 9, 25, 32; HIPAA 45 CFR §164.306, §164.312, §164.508; CCPA §1798; and Qatar PDPPL Articles 4, 6, 9, 13, and 14.

5. Consent

  • Data collection begins only after explicit informed consent.
  • You may withdraw consent at any time.
  • You may disable permissions via app or device settings.
  • Withdrawal does not affect previously lawful processing.

6. Data Storage & Security

  • End-to-end encryption (TLS)
  • Encrypted storage at rest
  • Pseudonymized user identifiers
  • Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
  • Audit logging
  • Secure cloud infrastructure

7. Data Sharing

Data may be shared only with authorized clinical researchers, affiliated hospital professionals, and contracted data processors under confidentiality agreements.

We do not sell or share data for advertising purposes.

8. Data Retention

  • Data retained only for the active research period.
  • Anonymized aggregated data may be retained longer.
  • Automatic deletion mechanisms apply.
  • You may request deletion at any time.

9. Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Request correction
  • Request deletion
  • Withdraw consent
  • Request data portability
  • Object or restrict processing

10. Children’s Privacy

This Application is not intended for minors unless part of a formally approved clinical study with legal guardian consent.

11. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated within the Application.
